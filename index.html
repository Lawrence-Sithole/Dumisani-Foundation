Our mission is to help children from disadvantaged backgrounds in Zimbabwe access quality education and enjoy sports activities. We do this by offering financial assistance for school fees, educational costs, food and transport. We also supply sports kits and equipment to encourage physical fitness and wellbeing. Furthermore, we support the construction of schools that serve the local community.

What We do


We provide sports kits and equipment to allow the children to play sports


We facilitate and celebrate cultural exchange.


The relief of poverty by identifying children from disadvantaged backgrounds in Zimbabwe, by providing financial support towards school fees and the costs of education.
